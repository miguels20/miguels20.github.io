About Me

I am currently a senior studying Computer Science and Mathematics here at Texas A&M University. I grew up just outside of Dallas in the town of Balch Springs and have lived there my whole life before coming down to College Station. During my final year in high school, I wasn't sure what exactly I wanted to do with my life. All I knew was that I loved nature and animals, but I was also pretty good at math and enjoyed messing around with technology. So, I was at a crossroads in my life. I ultimately chose Computer Science, but to this day have a desire to one day use my knowledge to help animals out in some way or another.

Personal Interests/Hobbies

Professional Interests
